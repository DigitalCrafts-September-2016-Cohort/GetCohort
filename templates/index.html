DigitalCrafts

DigitalCrafts is a 16-Week Coding Bootcamp in Atlanta and Houston. Their program covers 2 full web stacks over the course of 16 weeks, with a rotating elective in the evenings. Javascript, Node.js & the MEAN stack encompass some of the most cutting edge technologies today and are being used at companies like Google, LinkedIn, Netflix, Walmart, and PayPal. Python is an in-demand and versatile programming language widely used in both web development & the ever-growing field of data analytics.

The curriculum at DigitalCrafts is proven and their graduates work at great companies like Home Depot, New York Times, Car & Driver, LiveAction, Soltech, Market America, Kredible, Piksel, Greater Sum, Big Nerd Ranch, Narwhal, RiskAlyze, Army of Bees, BridalLive, Liaison Technologies, and more.
